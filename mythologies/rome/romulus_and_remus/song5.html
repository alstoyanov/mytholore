Brothers of the Blade

Story

This is the anthem of Romulus and Remus as warrior-brothers—the sons of Mars and wolves, raised by shepherds, hardened by chaos. Before the betrayal, before the bloodshed, they were united. Together they fought bandits, tyrants, and the fate that hunted them from birth.

This track is their battle cry: primal, noble, brutal. It captures their strength and their bond—one forged in flame and war.

After growing up unaware of their divine blood, Romulus and Remus became fierce warriors. They fought to restore their grandfather Numitor’s throne and led raids across the region. This track channels their legendary courage and fearlessness—the moment before destiny tears them apart.
