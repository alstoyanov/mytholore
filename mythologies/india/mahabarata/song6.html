Exile & Vengeance

Story

This song captures one of the most defining moments in the Mahabharata—the exile of the Pandavas and their burning vow of vengeance. ⚔️ The Story of Exile & Vengeance:

  • After being deceived in the game of dice, the Pandavas lose their kingdom, honor, and freedom.
  • Sent into twelve years of exile and one year of disguise, they are forced to live as outcasts, sharpening their rage for the inevitable war.
  • Draupadi, humiliated and unavenged, swears that she will not tie her hair until it is bathed in Dushasana’s blood.
  • Krishna, witnessing the depths of injustice, foretells a war that will drown the world in blood.
  • The Kurukshetra War is no longer a question of if—but when!
