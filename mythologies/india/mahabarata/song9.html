The Chakravyuha (Abhimanyu’s Last Stand)

Story

Among the most tragic and heroic moments in the Mahabharata, this song tells the story of Abhimanyu, the son of Arjuna, who fought alone against an army in the impenetrable Chakravyuha battle formation. Though he knew how to enter the labyrinth of death, he did not know the way out.

⚔️ The Tragic Fate of Abhimanyu:

  • 16 years old, yet standing as a warrior among kings.
  • Deceived into battle, trapped within the Chakravyuha while his allies were kept away.
  • Seven Kaurava generals, including Karna, Drona, and Duryodhana, surrounded him, breaking the rules of honorable combat.
  • Despite being outnumbered, he fought like a god, cutting down warriors, smashing chariots, and breaking their lines.
  • But betrayal and exhaustion took their toll—his weapons shattered, his armor torn, and still, he fought on with fists and fury.
  • Spears rained down, swords struck deep—yet Abhimanyu died a warrior, unbowed, unbroken, undefeated in spirit.
