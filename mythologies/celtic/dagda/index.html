Strings of the Gods

"Strings of the Gods" is a thunderous, mythic metal odyssey inspired by the legendary harp of Dagda, the divine instrument of the Tuatha Dé Danann. Forged in the Otherworld and imbued with supernatural power, its enchanted strings could command sleep, summon sorrow, and ignite war. Now, its music rises again—woven into a relentless, battle-forged album that brings Celtic mythology to life with blistering riffs, orchestral grandeur, and storytelling as old as time itself.

From the eerie birth of Uaithne in "Forged in the Otherworld", to the war drums of "Tuatha Rising", to the sorrowful lament of lost souls in "Echoes of Uaithne", this album is a journey through ancient battles, divine vengeance, and the unbreakable power of music. At its heart lies Dagda’s Harp, an instrument so powerful that even the monstrous Fomorians sought to claim it. Through crushing anthems like "The Fomorian Curse", the album explores the war between light and shadow, culminating in the triumphant final notes of "Harp of the Gods, Sword of the King."

Let the strings guide you. Let the legend consume you.
