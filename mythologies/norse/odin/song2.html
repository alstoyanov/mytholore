Mímir’s Price

Story

"One eye lost… yet I see all." ⚔️

Odin, the relentless seeker of wisdom, rides to Mímir’s Well, the sacred source of knowledge hidden beneath the roots of Yggdrasil. But wisdom is never free. To drink from its waters and gain the sight of gods, he must make the ultimate sacrifice—his own eye. In this moment, Odin embraces both knowledge and doom, seeing the fate of gods, the coming of Ragnarök, and the price all must pay for truth.

"Mímir’s Price" captures this legendary sacrifice in a crushing storm of thunderous riffs, battle hymns, and dark choirs, summoning the very essence of Norse myth and epic metal. This is the tale of the Wanderer, the One-Eyed King, the Lord of Ravens. This is Odin’s fate.
