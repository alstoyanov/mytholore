Riddles of the Giant

Story

"Fate and wisdom intertwined, in every whispered claim—

In the labyrinth of destiny, we play the endless game."

Odin, the Allfather, is not just a warrior but a seeker of forbidden knowledge. Disguised as a mortal wanderer, he enters the hall of Vafþrúðnir, the oldest and wisest of the giants, to challenge him in a battle of riddles—where the loser forfeits his life. Each question is a door to the secrets of creation, fate, and the end of the gods, as they duel with words instead of steel. But when Odin asks a question that only he can answer, the giant realizes his doom—his wisdom is great, but no one outsmarts the god of knowledge.
