The One-Eyed Seeker

Story

"He gave an eye, but gained the world." ⚔️

Step into the mythic realm with "The One-Eyed Seeker," a song inspired by Odin’s relentless pursuit of wisdom. From the storm-cloaked skies of Asgard to the shadowed roots of Yggdrasil, Odin wanders the Nine Realms in search of knowledge, power, and fate’s hidden truths.

This track brings to life one of the Allfather’s most legendary sacrifices—offering his eye to Mímir’s Well in exchange for the wisdom of the cosmos. But knowledge has a price, and Odin’s journey does not end there. Carving runes into reality, whispering to the dead, and walking the path of destiny, he is the eternal seeker of truth.
