Hammer of Storms

Hammer of the Storms is a thundering tribute to Thor, the storm-bringer and protector of gods and men in Norse mythology. Wielder of Mjölnir, son of Odin, and slayer of giants, Thor embodies raw strength, relentless courage, and the unyielding force of nature itself. This album captures that primal power in a maelstrom of sound—riffs that crash like rolling thunder, drums that strike like lightning splitting the sky, and vocals that roar with the fury of a god’s war cry. It is metal forged from storm clouds and runes, a hymn to the might that holds chaos at bay.

Each track channels a legend from the thunder god’s saga: Mjölnir Forged opens with molten aggression, summoning the hammer that shakes the Nine Realms. Jörmungandr Rising coils with ferocious tension, telling of Thor’s destined battle with the World Serpent. Giants’ Bane hammers forward in relentless fury, a tribute to Thor’s countless victories over the Jötnar. The Goat-Drawn Chariot races with unstoppable momentum, capturing the roaring skies as Thor rides across the heavens. And in Ragnarök Unleashed, the album reaches its apocalyptic peak—an earth-shattering anthem of final battle, where thunder meets the serpent and gods meet their doom.
