Rostam and Sohrab’s

Story

Rostam, renowned for his strength and valor, and Sohrab, a young and powerful warrior, are unaware of their relationship due to the separation of their mother, Tahmineh, and Rostam years before. As the two meet on the battlefield, they are forced into combat. They fight fiercely, with Rostam unaware that his opponent is his own blood.

In the climax of this tragic story, Rostam strikes Sohrab down in battle, only to realize, too late, that he has killed his own son. The pain of this realization is unbearable for Rostam, and he is left to mourn the loss of his child—his flesh and blood—caused by the very hands that gave him life.

This tale serves as a tragic reminder of the consequences of fate, war, and the unforeseen consequences of actions taken in the heat of battle. "Rostam and Sohrab" is not just a story of war, but a profound exploration of love, loss, and the inescapable reach of destiny.
