Rakhsh, the Steed of thunder

Story

"Rakhsh, the Steed of Thunder" brings to life the mythic bond between the legendary Persian hero Rostam and his mighty steed, Rakhsh. This song celebrates the birth and journey of Rakhsh, a horse unlike any other—fierce, untamed, and destined for greatness.

According to the Shahnameh of Ferdowsi, Rakhsh was no ordinary horse. Born under a mystical omen, he was chosen by Rostam, the hero who would tame him, and together they would carve their names into the annals of Persian legend. Rakhsh's bond with Rostam was forged in blood and battle, becoming an inseparable companion in countless wars, and a symbol of untamed power and loyalty.

From mountain peaks to the torrential battles of the battlefield, Rakhsh’s hooves were said to thunder like a storm, shaking the earth beneath him. His fury and strength were matched only by the will of his rider. Whether they faced dragons, demons, or treacherous kings, Rakhsh remained the embodiment of warrior spirit and divine destiny.

"Rakhsh, the Steed of Thunder" encapsulates the mythological essence of this mighty horse—a creature of lightning and fire, an eternal symbol of Persia’s strength and glory. Join us on this epic journey through time, as we honor the warrior steed that rides into battle beside the greatest hero Persia has ever known.
