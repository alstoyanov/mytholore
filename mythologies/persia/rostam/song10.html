A Hero’s curse

Story

Rostam, revered across Persia for his feats of strength and bravery, is also burdened by the curse of heroism. The more victories he wins, the more he loses. His battles bring glory, but they also come with a heavy cost—whether it’s the loss of loved ones, the toll on his body and soul, or the tragic consequences of his actions. Over time, this curse of heroism consumes him, reminding him that no hero’s journey is without a price.

The song echoes the internal conflict of a hero who can never escape the shadows of his past—each victory a reminder of the losses that come with it. Whether fighting monsters, kings, or tyrants, Rostam's triumphs are overshadowed by the burden they place on him. "A Hero’s Curse" is a poignant reflection on the price of destiny, the emptiness of fame, and the inevitability of fate in Persian mythology.
