Shadows of betrayal

Story

The Shahnameh tells of how Rostam, once a man of honor and strength, is betrayed by his own allies, leading to a tragic sequence of events that ultimately shakes the foundations of his courage and trust. Betrayal enters Rostam’s life through his closest companions and those he swore loyalty to. It causes him internal conflict, grief, and, eventually, the death of those who wronged him. Despite his rage, Rostam’s path is one of sacrifice and redemption, as he battles not just enemies, but his own disillusionment. The song portrays how betrayal weaves its shadow through the life of a hero—its consequences unshakable, its scars eternal.

Rostam’s struggle to understand the pain of betrayal and to regain his sense of purpose resonates with anyone who has ever been hurt by those they trusted. The song asks: Can a hero ever truly be free from the shadows of the past?

This song explores the darker side of Rostam’s story, focusing on the emotional and psychological toll that betrayal takes on a warrior’s heart.
