Eternal thunder

Story

Rostam, revered across Persia for his unmatched strength and courage, faces a fate crueler than any battlefield. The song highlights Rostam’s rise to legend, his undying courage, and the tragic twist of his life. In the final moments of his journey, Rostam unknowingly battles his son, Sohrab, a hero in his own right, leading to a heartbreaking and fatal confrontation. This tragic tale unfolds with a poisoned spear and a final battle that ends with Rostam’s fall, leaving Persia to mourn the loss of its greatest hero.

The song’s narrative emphasizes the sorrow and complexity of Rostam’s story—the unbreakable thunder of his legacy echoing through the ages. The female voice telling the story adds depth, portraying Rostam’s strength but also the tragic cost of heroism, illustrating the idea that even the mightiest warriors must face the weight of their actions.
