Order of the Flame

Story

Order of the Flame is the final declaration of Marduk, who ascends to cosmic kingship after defeating Tiamat in the Enuma Elish. He establishes order from chaos, hangs the stars, creates time, and restructures the divine hierarchy. His reign begins not with peace, but with structure, rule, and fire.
