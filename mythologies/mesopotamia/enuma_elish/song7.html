Divine Tribunal

Story

Divine Tribunal tells the moment in the Enuma Elish when the gods, facing annihilation by Tiamat and her army, gather in fear and solemn desperation. Only Marduk steps forward to fight her — but in return, he demands the throne of the cosmos. The gods agree. This is the celestial courtroom where fate changes forever.
