Voices of Clay

Story

Voices of Clay is the awakening of humanity, created from the blood of the slain god Kingu and molded by Ea to serve the gods. In the Enuma Elish, humans are made for labor — to uphold the divine order. But in this track, they speak. They question. They remember what runs in their veins: a spark of chaos and a whisper of rebellion.
