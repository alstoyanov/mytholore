Forge the Beasts

Story

Forge the Beasts roars from the throat of Kingu, the chosen consort of Tiamat and bearer of the Tablet of Destinies in the Enuma Elish. Gifted divine authority, he becomes general of her monstrous army — crafting beasts and demons to wage war on the gods. This song captures the ritual of summoning chaos and the defiant rhythm of war.
