Wrath of the Dragon Queen

Story

Wrath of the Dragon Queen unleashes the fury of Tiamat, the primordial goddess of saltwater and chaos, following the betrayal and death of Apsu in the Enuma Elish. Once the mother of gods, she becomes a force of divine vengeance, birthing monstrous legions and declaring war upon her own descendants. This track marks her transformation from cosmic womb to world-ending wrath.
