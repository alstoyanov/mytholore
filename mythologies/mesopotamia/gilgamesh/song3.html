Battle of Uruk

Story

"Battle of Uruk" is an intense metal anthem that brings to life one of the most iconic moments in Mesopotamian mythology: the fierce battle between Gilgamesh and Enkidu. The song captures the raw power and unstoppable force of two mighty beings clashing in the streets of Uruk. As the battle rages, pride and honor are tested, leading to a powerful transformation—one that forges an unbreakable bond between the two warriors.

In this song, Gilgamesh, the arrogant king of Uruk, faces off against Enkidu, a wild man from the forest. What begins as a battle for dominance soon turns into a legendary friendship, changing the course of their fates forever. Through thunderous riffs, fierce vocals, and anthemic choruses, "Battle of Uruk" is an exploration of power, rivalry, and the creation of a legendary bond.
