Ishtar's Wrath

Story

"Ishtar’s Wrath" is a powerful and intense metal anthem, told from the perspective of the vengeful Ishtar, the goddess of love, war, and fertility. Rejected by Gilgamesh after offering him her affections, Ishtar unleashes her fury upon him and the earth, summoning the Bull of Heaven to exact her vengeance. This song channels her anger, pride, and divine wrath, showcasing her determination to make Gilgamesh pay for his rejection.

With a fierce female lead, "Ishtar’s Wrath" explores the goddess's emotions in the wake of her humiliation and the overwhelming power she commands. The music intensifies her rage and highlights the destructive consequences of defying a goddess. Through dynamic vocals and crushing metal riffs, the song brings Ishtar’s fury to life, making it a must-listen for fans of mythology and heavy music.
