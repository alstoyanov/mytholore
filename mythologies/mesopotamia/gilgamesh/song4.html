Humbaba's Curse

Story

"Humbaba’s Curse" is a powerful metal track inspired by the ancient myth of Gilgamesh and his fateful encounter with Humbaba, the guardian of the Cedar Forest. In this epic tale, Gilgamesh and Enkidu defeat the mighty Humbaba, but their victory comes at a steep cost: a deadly curse cast by the guardian in his final moments. As the gods watch from above, the curse begins to unfold, promising eternal retribution.

This song captures the dark and brooding atmosphere of Humbaba’s curse, blending haunting melodies and intense metal riffs to illustrate the growing tension as Gilgamesh and Enkidu’s journey takes a darker turn. With both male and female vocals, the song contrasts the raw power and haunting fate that now hangs over the heroes as they face the consequences of their actions.
