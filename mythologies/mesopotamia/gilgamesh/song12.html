The Eternal City

Story

"The Eternal City" is an evocative metal song inspired by the Epic of Gilgamesh, focusing on the rise and inevitable fall of Uruk, the great city that Gilgamesh built as a symbol of his power. The song explores the themes of ambition, pride, and the passage of time, illustrating how even the most grandiose human creations are bound to fade. Through a combination of heavy guitar riffs, male and female vocals, and haunting lyrics, "The Eternal City" emphasizes the tension between the desire for immortality and the inevitability of mortality.

As the city that Gilgamesh envisioned to last forever begins to crumble under the weight of time, this song reflects on the fleeting nature of human achievement and the humbling realization that no creation, no matter how mighty, can defy time’s relentless march. Fans of mythology, metal, and emotional storytelling will appreciate this journey into the heart of an ancient tale, told through powerful music.
