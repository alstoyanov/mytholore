The Serpent's Betrayal

Story

"The Serpent’s Betrayal" is a powerful and emotional metal song inspired by the Epic of Gilgamesh, chronicling the tragic moment when Gilgamesh’s quest for immortality comes to a devastating end. After traveling far and wide to retrieve the Plant of Youth, Gilgamesh is deceived and betrayed by a serpent who steals the life-giving plant from his grasp.

This track explores the themes of loss, betrayal, and the harshness of fate. With striking male and female vocals, "The Serpent’s Betrayal" delves into Gilgamesh's emotional devastation as his hopes of eternal life are dashed by the cunning of the serpent. The song captures the feeling of helplessness as the plant slips away, forever out of reach, and the reality of mortality sets in.
