Utnapishtim’s Warning

Story

"Utnapishtim’s Warning" is a haunting metal ballad inspired by the Epic of Gilgamesh, exploring the somber wisdom imparted by Utnapishtim to the great king Gilgamesh. Having lived through the great flood and been granted immortality, Utnapishtim reveals the futility of seeking eternal life and warns Gilgamesh about the inevitability of death.

The track, with its powerful male and female vocals, carries the weight of this mythological wisdom, blending melancholic melodies with crushing metal riffs. As Utnapishtim tells of his own losses, his struggle with immortality, and the painful truth of fate, listeners are reminded of the fragile nature of life and the relentless passage of time.
