King of Uruk

Story

"King of Uruk" brings the ancient myth of Gilgamesh to life through the power of metal music. Drawing from the rich tapestry of Mesopotamian mythology, this track encapsulates the legendary strength, arrogance, and eventual self-awareness of Gilgamesh, the mighty king of Uruk. As he stands atop his kingdom, invincible and unmatched, his rule is challenged by forces beyond his control: the wrath of the gods and the rise of a powerful companion, Enkidu.

In the ancient city, Gilgamesh’s quest for glory and immortality pushes him to confront not only external enemies but also the very nature of his existence. This song captures his struggle, his victories, and his eventual realization that true power lies not in physical might, but in the legacy he leaves behind.
