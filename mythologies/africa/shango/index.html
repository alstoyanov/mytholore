Thunder Ascended

Thunder Ascended strikes with the raw force of Shango, the Yoruba orisha of thunder, lightning, fire, and drums. Revered as a warrior-king, a bringer of justice, and a storm that cannot be contained, Shango’s myth reverberates with power and passion. This album channels his fiery spirit into a tempest of sound—guitars that crack like lightning, drums that thunder like the sacred bàtá, and vocals that roar with the fury of storms rolling across the savannah.

Each track embodies an aspect of the god’s legend: The Double-Headed Axe swings with sharp, cutting riffs, invoking Shango’s sacred weapon, symbol of war and authority. Storm of Oyo unleashes relentless rhythm, echoing his reign as the great king who became a god. The Wrath of Fire blazes with molten intensity, conjuring his destructive side, while Dance of the Drums fuses primal groove and heaviness, a celebration of the sacred rhythms that call down his presence. The album reaches its peak with Thunder Ascended, a triumphant anthem of transformation, immortalizing Shango’s rise from mortal king to divine storm.

Thunder Ascended is more than metal—it is invocation. It is the voice of thunder rolling across the skies, the crackle of fire cleansing the earth, and the pulse of ancestral drums that refuse to fade. Through sheer force and mythic grandeur, the album brings Shango’s power into the modern age, a storm reborn in distortion and flame. This is the sound of justice, passion, and divine wrath—the storm that ascends, eternal.
