Hornet trap

Story

"Hornet Trap" brings to life one of Anansi's most cunning feats, as the legendary trickster god uses wit and cleverness to trap a swarm of hornets and win his ultimate prize — the stories of the world.

In this fierce and fast-paced track, Anansi sets a clever trap for the hornets, tricking them into a pot where they are sealed inside, unable to escape. As the storm rages around them, the hornets fall into the trap, and Anansi claims victory once again through his unmatched cunning. This metal anthem channels the energy of the storm, the buzzing fury of the hornets, and Anansi’s perfect strategy. With explosive riffs and a relentless beat, "Hornet Trap" captures the thrill of outsmarting even the most dangerous creatures.

The song draws from Ashanti and Akan mythology, where Anansi’s cleverness knows no bounds. He demonstrates the power of strategy and intelligence over brute force, showing that even the most formidable challenges can be overcome with a well-laid plan.
