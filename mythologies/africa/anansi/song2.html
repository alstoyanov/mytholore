Albums/ African Mythologies/ Web of the Tricster

Story

This heavy-hitting metal track brings to life the legendary Anansi — a master manipulator, a weaver of webs, and a cunning trickster who outwits gods, kings, and all who dare cross his path. With every beat and riff, Anansi is on the hunt, spinning his web of lies and deceit with the precision of a spider, all while laughing in the face of those who underestimate him.

Inspired by the rich Ashanti and Akan myths, "Eight-Legged Schemer" encapsulates the essence of Anansi’s mischievous nature, his ability to turn the tables with a few clever words, and his never-ending dance with fate. The track channels the essence of his trickster power, blending brutal guitar riffs and rhythmic beats with the cunning and stealth of Anansi's world-spanning webs.

Anansi’s legacy endures in tales where brains, not brawn, conquer the day — and this track celebrates the eternal trickster who bends reality, one thread at a time. Whether you’re into metal, myth, or powerful storytelling, "Eight-Legged Schemer" is sure to leave you tangled in its web.
