The Lion’s Share

Story

"The Lion’s Share" is a powerful, energetic track that tells the story of Anansi’s cunning victory over the mighty lion, one of the fiercest creatures in the jungle. The song captures Anansi’s cleverness and his ability to outsmart the strongest of foes, using nothing but his intelligence and wit.

In this song, Anansi faces the lion — a symbol of raw power and brute strength. But through his trickery, Anansi turns the tables, claiming the lion’s strength as his own. The music blends heavy guitar riffs and pounding rhythms with moments of tension and triumph, reflecting the battle of wit versus might. The victory over the lion is not just a win over one creature, but a representation of intelligence triumphing over strength.

Inspired by Ashanti and Akan mythology, "The Lion’s Share" embodies Anansi’s role as the trickster god who uses guile and wisdom to conquer overwhelming odds. It’s a reminder that even the mightiest can fall, and the cleverest will always find a way to win.
