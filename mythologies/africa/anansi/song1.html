Beast of Nemea

Story

The First Labor of Hercules was to slay the Nemean Lion, a monstrous beast with impenetrable skin that terrorized the land of Nemea. No weapon could pierce its hide, so Hercules had to strangle it with his bare hands after trapping it in a cave. After killing the lion, he skinned it using its own claws and wore its pelt as an invincible cloak, which became one of his most iconic symbols.
