The Feast of fools

Story

"The Feast of Fools" is a high-energy metal track that brings to life the legendary story of Anansi, the trickster god, as he attends a grand feast with the gods — and turns the tables on them with his cunning wit.

In this song, Anansi is invited to the gods' banquet, where they mock him for his small size and perceived weakness. But Anansi, ever the trickster, uses his intelligence and deception to win the gods' respect and gain the upper hand. As he feigns hunger and plays the fool, the gods fall for his clever tricks and are outsmarted at their own feast. The music captures Anansi’s strategic mind, the tension of the gods’ mockery, and his eventual triumph.

Rooted in Ashanti and Akan mythology, "The Feast of Fools" celebrates Anansi’s cleverness and resilience. This track embodies the eternal trickster who proves that wit and guile can defeat strength and pride, turning even the gods into his pawns. A reminder that in the world of myth, it’s not always the strongest who win — but the most cunning.
