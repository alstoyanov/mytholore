Fangs and wisdom

Story

"Fangs and Wisdom" is an electrifying metal track inspired by one of Anansi's greatest victories in African folklore — his cunning defeat of the mighty python.

In this powerful anthem, Anansi faces off against the serpent, using his wit and wisdom to defeat a creature of unimaginable strength. As the python strikes with its deadly fangs, Anansi spins a web of lies and tricks that leads the serpent into a trap. The music captures the tension, danger, and eventual triumph of the trickster god, blending powerful guitar riffs and pounding drums to represent Anansi's intelligence triumphing over brute strength.

Drawing from the Ashanti and Akan myths, "Fangs and Wisdom" celebrates the age-old message that intellect and wisdom often win the day against overwhelming force. Anansi’s story of defeating the python is one of cunning over brawn, a tale that shows how brains, not brawn, make a true hero.
