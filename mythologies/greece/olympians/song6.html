Ares: Blood for Glory

Story

"Blood for Glory" unleashes the raw, unfiltered voice of Ares—the Greek god who thrives not in strategy, but in the brutal chaos of battle. This song explores his disdain for diplomacy, his rivalry with Athena, and his sacred connection to bloodshed and valor. Speaking in his own words, Ares justifies his role in the pantheon: not as a monster, but as the necessary force that crowns heroes and decides empires.

Ares is the Greek god of war, representing the violent, untamed, and destructive aspects of conflict. Unlike Athena, who governs strategic warfare, Ares embodies bloodlust, fury, and the thrill of combat. Often scorned by the other Olympians, he is still revered in the chaos of war, where strength and survival rule.
