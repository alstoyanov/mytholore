Hecate: Witchflame

Story

"Witchflame" gives voice to Hecate, the enigmatic goddess of magic, thresholds, and the unseen. She is the light in the dark, the guide between life and death, and the mistress of shadowed power. This final track honors her as the force who illuminates hidden truths, walks with the dead, and empowers those who dwell in mystery and moonlight.

Hecate is the Greek goddess of magic, witchcraft, necromancy, and the crossroads. She is often depicted carrying torches, accompanied by black dogs, and shown with three faces to symbolize her dominion over past, present, and future. Revered by witches and feared by mortals, Hecate is a liminal figure—powerful, wise, and unbound by the laws of Olympus.
