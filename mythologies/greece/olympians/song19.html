Nike: Wings of Victory

Story

"Wings of Victory" is a triumphant anthem told through the voice of Nike, divine embodiment of success and glory. In battlefields and arenas, she appears unseen, crowning the worthy. This song explores her eternal presence in every triumph, from heroic last stands to quiet personal wins. Victory, she reminds us, is not forged by strength alone—but by the moment she chooses to bless.

Nike is the Greek goddess of victory, closely associated with both war and peaceful competition. Often depicted with wings, she flies swiftly across the battlefield to reward the worthy. She frequently appears beside Athena in myth and was honored by both soldiers and athletes. Nike is the spirit behind every lasting achievement and every name written in glory.
