Aphrodite: Beautiful Curse

Story

"Beautiful Curse" is the voice of Aphrodite in her most divine and dangerous form. This track explores how beauty, love, and desire can lead to devastation. From the Trojan War sparked by her gift to Paris, to her affairs with gods and mortals, Aphrodite weaves a path of passion and ruin. She is not a comfort—she is a force. The song captures the seductive power of a goddess who gives pleasure wrapped in peril.

Aphrodite is the Greek goddess of love, beauty, passion, and sensuality. Born from the sea foam after the fall of Uranus, she is often accompanied by doves and roses. She had many lovers, including Ares and Adonis, and was married to Hephaestus. While she inspires love, she also ignites jealousy, war, and heartbreak—her influence is as dangerous as it is irresistible.
