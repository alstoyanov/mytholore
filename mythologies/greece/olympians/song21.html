Tyche: Wheel in My Hands

Story

“Wheel in My Hands” is a dynamic, myth-inspired song sung from the perspective of Tyche, the Greek goddess of fortune, fate, and chance. Often depicted holding a wheel or cornucopia, Tyche represents the unpredictable nature of life’s highs and lows.

Tyche is the Greek goddess of fortune, luck, and the unpredictable twists of fate. Often depicted holding a cornucopia overflowing with riches and a wheel symbolizing the constant change of destiny, she governs both prosperity and disaster without bias. Tyche embodies the randomness of life’s outcomes, steering the lives of individuals and the fates of entire cities according to her whims. Revered especially during times of uncertainty, she was seen as both a protector and a capricious force beyond human control, reminding mortals that success and ruin often come without warning.

Whether you’ve praised her in triumph or cursed her in defeat, Tyche remains the hidden hand behind the game of life. She doesn’t play favorites — her gifts are sudden, her withdrawals swift.
