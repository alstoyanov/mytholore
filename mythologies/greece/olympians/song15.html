Nemesis: Justice with a Blade

Story

"Justice with a Blade" gives voice to Nemesis, the relentless force of divine retribution. This song captures the silent, calculating vengeance of the goddess who punishes hubris and restores balance to the moral order. She does not act out of rage, but necessity—ensuring that no injustice goes unanswered, and no excessive pride remains unchecked.

Nemesis is the Greek goddess of retribution, balance, and vengeance. She punishes arrogance against the gods (hubris), delivers fate, and ensures that excessive fortune is countered with correction. Represented with a sword and scales, Nemesis is feared by mortals and respected even among gods. She is the embodiment of cosmic balance and karmic justice.
