The Labyrinth's Curse

The Labyrinth’s Curse plunges into the darkness of Greek mythology, retelling the tragic saga of the Minotaur, the monstrous son of Minos’s deceit and Poseidon’s wrath. Born of curse and shame, imprisoned in Daedalus’s endless maze, and fed on human sacrifice, the Minotaur embodies the terror that lurks at the heart of power. This album transforms that myth into a crushing soundscape—riffs that twist and coil like stone corridors, drums that pound like footsteps in the dark, and vocals that roar with both beastly fury and human anguish.

Each track conjures a chapter of the tale: Poseidon’s Wrath seethes with molten heaviness, telling of the curse that sparked the monster’s birth. The Maze of Daedalus spirals with intricate riffs and shifting rhythms, a sonic labyrinth where no path leads to light. Blood of the Innocents rages with relentless brutality, embodying the sacrifices sent into the maze. The Hero of Athens surges with sharpened intensity, as Theseus descends into darkness. Finally, Thread of Ariadne rises as a haunting yet triumphant anthem, weaving hope into despair as the labyrinth’s curse is broken by cunning and courage.
