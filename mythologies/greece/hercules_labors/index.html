Labors of the Titanborn

This concept album reimagines the Twelve Labors of Heracles as a crushing epic of mythic metal. Each track becomes a saga of fury and triumph: towering riffs evoke the Hydra’s writhing heads, thunderous drums drive the hunt for the Ceryneian Hind, and relentless breakdowns mirror the chaos of the Erymanthian Boar’s capture. The music moves between brutal heaviness and soaring melodic passages, capturing both the hero’s raw strength and his moments of cunning and endurance.

Lyrically, the album blends ancient imagery with modern intensity, painting Heracles not just as a demigod but as a symbol of human struggle against impossible odds. From the filth-cleansing flood of the Augean Stables to the cosmic weight of bringing Cerberus from Hades, the record unfolds as a journey through darkness, wrath, and redemption—an unrelenting testament to power, perseverance, and the cost of glory.
