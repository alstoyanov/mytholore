Medea’s betrayals

Story

Medea’s Betrayal is a powerful, symphonic metal ballad that tells the tragic story of Medea, the sorceress who sacrificed everything for Jason—only to be cast aside for another. Fueled by heartbreak and vengeance, Medea’s wrath becomes legendary, sealing Jason’s fate in sorrow and blood.
