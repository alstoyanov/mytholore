The Dragon’s Curse

Story

The Dragon’s Curse is a brooding, intense metal anthem recounting Jason’s final trial before claiming the Golden Fleece. In the depths of Colchis, a colossal serpent—gifted with eternal wakefulness—guards the sacred relic. No blade can pierce its hide, no warrior can stand before its gaze.

But the gods demand sacrifice. With Medea’s sorcery, Jason attempts to lull the beast into sleep, but curses are never lifted without consequence.
