The Return to Iolcus

Story

The Return to Iolcus is an epic metal anthem telling the final chapter of Jason’s quest. After braving the perils of Colchis, stealing the Golden Fleece, and defying gods and kings, Jason returns home—only to find that victory is never without consequence. His home is not as he left it, and the throne he seeks is one built on betrayal, blood, and divine wrath.
