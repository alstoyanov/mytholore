The Harpies' Wrath

Story

The Harpies’ Wrath is the next furious chapter in our mythological metal epic, The Quest for the Golden Fleece. This track tells the tale of Jason and the Argonauts encountering the cursed king Phineus, tormented by the monstrous Harpies—ravenous creatures sent by the gods to steal his every meal, leaving him in endless suffering.

With relentless drums, shredding guitars, and piercing vocals, this song embodies the chaos of the winged furies as they descend from the heavens to unleash destruction. The Argonauts stand their ground, but only divine intervention can break the curse.
