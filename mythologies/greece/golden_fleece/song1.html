The Prophecy

Story

The Prophecy (Intro) is the haunting opening track to our concept album, The Quest for the Golden Fleece. This symphonic metal masterpiece draws inspiration from ancient Greek mythology, setting the stage for the epic tale of Jason and his legendary quest to obtain the Golden Fleece.

The song introduces the prophecy that will guide Jason’s journey, delivered through sweeping orchestral melodies and powerful vocals. As the stars themselves seem to dictate the hero’s fate, the track serves as a foreboding call to adventure, setting the tone for the challenges, betrayals, and divine interventions that will follow.
