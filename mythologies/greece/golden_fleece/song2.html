Gathering of the Argonauts

Story

Gathering of the Argonauts is the powerful second track from our album The Quest for the Golden Fleece. This anthem celebrates the legendary assembly of heroes, led by Jason, as they answer the call to embark on the greatest adventure of Greek mythology.

As they prepare to set sail for the Golden Fleece, the song captures the essence of camaraderie, courage, and determination. With soaring guitar riffs and symphonic arrangements, the song introduces the mighty warriors who join the quest, from Hercules' unmatched strength to Orpheus' enchanting music.
