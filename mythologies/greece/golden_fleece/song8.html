The Bull of Bronze

Story

The Bull of Bronze is a blazing metal anthem recounting Jason’s first trial in Colchis. To prove himself worthy of the Golden Fleece, Jason must tame the monstrous, fire-breathing bulls—beasts forged by Hephaestus himself. With steel hooves that shatter the earth and flames that reduce men to ash, the bulls are an unstoppable force of divine fury.

With thunderous drums, blistering guitar solos, and primal vocals, this track captures the raw power and intensity of Jason’s battle against the beast. With only Medea’s dark sorcery to aid him, will Jason control the raging inferno, or be consumed by its wrath?
