The Golden Fleece

Prepare to immerse yourself in a world of myth, power, and betrayal as Mytholore unleashes their latest symphonic metal masterpiece: The Quest for the Golden Fleece. This concept album takes you through the legendary tale of Jason and the Argonauts, as they sail across treacherous seas, battle gods and monsters, and face the ultimate trials to claim the fabled Golden Fleece.

With bone-crushing riffs, soaring orchestrations, and vocal performances that shift between haunting melodies and fiery power, each track breathes life into the ancient myths. From Medea's betrayal to the dragon's curse, this album captures the dark magic, sorrow, and heroism of the quest, transporting you to a time when gods controlled fate, and the line between heroism and tragedy was razor-thin.

The story unfolds in 13 epic tracks, each one a chapter of the legend, showcasing the highs and lows of the heroes, their trials, and their inevitable destinies. Whether you seek glory or redemption, this album will take you on a ride through ancient lore that will resonate with fans of metal and mythology alike.
