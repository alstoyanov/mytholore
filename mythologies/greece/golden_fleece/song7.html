Trials of the Heart

Story

Trials of the Heart takes you deep into the mystical love story of Medea and Jason, the tale of an enchanted sorceress who risks everything to help the hero claim the Golden Fleece. In this powerful duet, Medea confesses her growing love for Jason while revealing the treacherous trials he must overcome to succeed in his quest.

Through haunting melodies and epic symphonic metal riffs, this song captures the tension between destiny and love, with Medea’s dark magic and Jason’s determination entwined in an unforgettable journey. As the trials unfold, Medea’s sacrifice becomes clear, and Jason’s fate hangs in the balance.
