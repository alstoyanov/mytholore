Across the Stormy Seas

Story

Across the Stormy Seas is the third epic chapter in our mythological metal saga, The Quest for the Golden Fleece. This track captures the perilous journey of Jason and the Argonauts as they sail through raging tempests, facing the wrath of Poseidon and the merciless sea.

Thunderous drums and crashing guitars echo the fury of the ocean, while haunting melodies invoke the struggle between mortals and gods. Inspired by ancient Greek mythology, this song tells the tale of a crew bound by fate, their courage tested against forces beyond their control.
