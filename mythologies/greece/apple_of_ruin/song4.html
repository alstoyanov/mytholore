Wisdom’s Blade

Story

As Paris prepares to judge between the goddesses, Athena, goddess of strategic war and divine intellect, offers him a gift unlike any other: eternal victory. She promises wisdom in combat, foresight in council, and unmatched dominance through clarity and thought.

Unlike the others, she offers no kingdom, no seduction. Instead, she offers understanding, strategy, and the power to control fate itself through reason.

This track is Athena’s voice—measured, brilliant, and fierce. She is the spear behind every empire, the blade behind every throne.
