Trial of the Gods

Story

The heavens tremble, the gods assemble, and the fate of Egypt hangs in the balance. Trial of the Gods is a cinematic and powerful anthem, telling the story of the final judgment of Horus and Seth, where the gods decide who will rule the throne of Osiris. Before the divine scales of Ma’at, truth will shine, and only one will emerge victorious.

With thundering war drums, ethereal choirs, and ancient Egyptian flutes, this song captures the tension, grandeur, and fate of the ultimate celestial trial. As Osiris, Anubis, and Ra watch in judgment, the sands of time shift, and destiny is sealed.

This song is inspired by the legendary trial of Horus and Seth, where the gods convene to decide the rightful ruler of Egypt. The heart is weighed against the feather of Ma’at, and only the worthy will rise. It is a tale of justice, divine law, and the final reckoning of an age-old conflict.
