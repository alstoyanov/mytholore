Whispers of the desert

Story

Step into the sands of ancient Egypt and witness the rise of betrayal in Seth of the Desert, a haunting and mysterious song inspired by the legend of Osiris and Seth. As Seth's jealousy grows, the desert winds carry whispers of fate, ambition, and treachery, foreshadowing the fall of a golden age.

This track captures the brooding intensity of Seth's envy toward Osiris, blending deep percussion, eerie flutes, and haunting chants to create an atmosphere of impending doom. Feel the creeping tension as the sands shift and destiny unfolds.
