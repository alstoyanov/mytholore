Magic of the moon

Story

Beneath the silver glow of the ancient moon, magic stirs in the desert night. Magic of the Moon is a haunting and mystical ballad inspired by Isis’s sacred ritual to bring Osiris back to life. With the gods as silent witnesses and the stars as her only guide, she weaves a spell that defies fate itself.

Featuring celestial choirs, shimmering harps, and deep, hypnotic percussion, this song blends sorrow, longing, and divine power. It is a tale of unbreakable love, whispered through time by the winds of the Nile.

Inspired by the Egyptian legend of Isis and Osiris, where Isis, in the wake of betrayal, calls upon the power of the moon to reunite with her lost love. Through sacred spells and ancient rites, she weaves a path between life and death, refusing to let the gods decide her fate.
