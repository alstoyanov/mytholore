The Coffin’s curse

Story

The night is silent, the halls are bright with celebration—but in the shadows, betrayal is set in motion. The Coffin’s Curse is a dark and dramatic song that tells the chilling tale of Seth’s ultimate act of treachery, trapping Osiris inside a coffin and casting him into the Nile. This is the moment where a golden age crumbles, where envy twists into murder, and where the whispers of fate seal a king’s doom.

This song is inspired by the Egyptian myth of Osiris’s betrayal, where Seth’s jealousy leads him to construct a beautifully crafted coffin, tricking Osiris into lying inside before sealing it shut and casting it into the Nile. It is the first act in a legendary cycle of death, resurrection, and revenge
