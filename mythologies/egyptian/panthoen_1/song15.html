Khonsu: Moonlit Traveler

Story

Float into celestial serenity and lunar mysticism with “Moonlit Traveler”, a progressive metal journey that pays homage to Khonsu, Egypt’s sacred moon god, healer, and protector of nighttime voyagers.

Who is Khonsu?

Khonsu (sometimes spelled Khons or Khonsou) is the moon god of ancient Egypt, closely associated with time, healing, and protection during the night. As the divine child of Amun and Mut, he rides the skies in the moon's glow, marking time and influencing the rhythms of body and spirit.

He was worshipped as a protector of travelers and children, as well as a god of restorative power and divine guidance. His image sometimes appears with a side-lock of youth and a full lunar disk upon his head, marking him as both ancient and eternal.
