About Mytholore

Mytholore is not just a band — it’s a dream forged in the heart of a grown-up kid who never stopped loving myths, legends, and the magic of storytelling. Born from a lifelong fascination with ancient tales, heroic epics, and forgotten gods, Mytholore is a passion project where music becomes a bridge to the timeless worlds of mythology.

Created by someone who humbly admits to having little formal talent in music, but an enormous, unstoppable love for the stories that shaped cultures and imaginations for centuries. Thanks to the empowering tools of artificial intelligence, Mytholore exists as a testament to what’s possible when technology meets passion. Through AI-assisted composition, production, and creativity, what once felt impossible is now a reality — an outlet for giving these ancient tales a new voice in the modern world.

Every song, every album is a labor of love: an attempt to honor the myths that inspired it, while adding a personal layer of interpretation and feeling. This isn’t about perfection or mainstream fame — it’s about connection. It’s about sharing wonder, awe, and the excitement of hearing a story retold through heavy riffs, haunting melodies, and epic soundscapes.

Mytholore invites you to join this journey, whether you’re a fellow myth-lover, a metal fan, or simply someone searching for meaning and magic in music. Thank you for listening, for supporting, and for keeping the stories alive.
