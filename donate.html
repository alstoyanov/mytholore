Support Mytholore

Mytholore wasn’t created with financial goals in mind. This project exists purely from a love of myths, legends, and the timeless power of storytelling through music. There’s no donation button here, because the greatest support you can give is simple: share the love of myths with others.

The best way to support Mytholore is to subscribe on your favorite streaming platform, listen to the music, and recommend it to friends who might enjoy these stories reborn in sound.

Mytholore is available on most streaming platforms, and has an active YouTube channel where the myths continue to come alive.

Your support through listening, subscribing, and sharing keeps the stories alive and spreads their magic.
